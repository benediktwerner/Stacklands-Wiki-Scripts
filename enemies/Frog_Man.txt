'''Frog Man''' is a [[:Category:Hostile Mobs|red]] [[Cards|Card]] in [[Stacklands]]. In the [[Cardopedia]], it is categorized as a [[:Category:Mobs|Mob]].

==How to Spawn==
*By exploring [[Catacombs]] (10%)
*From ''The Armory'' (6%), ''Explorers'' (6%), or ''Advanced Archipelago'' (7%) [[Card Packs]]
*From [[Strange Portal]] or [[Rare Portal]] starting [[Moon]] 16

==Possible Equipment==
*'''Hand''': [[Blowpipe]]

==Drops==
*If wearing equipment that is uncraftable or hasn't been found yet and no other mob has dropped any equipement in the current fight, one such worn equipment is dropped instead
*[[Blowpipe]] (11%, only if no other equipment has been dropped yet in the current fight)
*[[Goop]] (33%)
*[[Frog Helmet]] (11%, only if no other equipment has been dropped yet in the current fight)
*[[Hammer]] (22%, only if no other equipment has been dropped yet in the current fight)
*[[Pickaxe]] (22%, only if no other equipment has been dropped yet in the current fight)
