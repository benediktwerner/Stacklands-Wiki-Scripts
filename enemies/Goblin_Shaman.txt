'''Goblin Shaman''' is a [[:Category:Hostile Mobs|red]] [[Cards|Card]] in [[Stacklands]]. In the [[Cardopedia]], it is categorized as a [[:Category:Mobs|Mob]].

==How to Spawn==
*By exploring [[Catacombs]] (15%)
*From ''The Armory'' (12%) or ''Explorers'' (12%) [[Card Packs]]
*From [[Strange Portal]] or [[Rare Portal]] starting [[Moon]] 16

==Possible Equipment==
*'''Hand''': [[Bone Staff]], [[Magic Wand]], or [[Magic Blade]]

==Drops==
*If wearing equipment that is uncraftable or hasn't been found yet and no other mob has dropped any equipement in the current fight, one such worn equipment is dropped instead
*[[Magic Dust]] (75%)
*[[Bone]] (25%)
