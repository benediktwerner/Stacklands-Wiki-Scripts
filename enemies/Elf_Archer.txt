'''Elf Archer''' is a [[:Category:Hostile Mobs|red]] [[Cards|Card]] in [[Stacklands]]. In the [[Cardopedia]], it is categorized as a [[:Category:Mobs|Mob]].

==How to Spawn==
*From [[Strange Portal]] or [[Rare Portal]] starting [[Moon]] 24
*By visiting the [[Dark Forest]]

==Possible Equipment==
*'''Head''': [[Helmet]]
*'''Hand''': [[Bow]] or [[Crossbow]]
*'''Body''': [[Chainmail Armor]] or [[Quiver]]

==Drops==
*If wearing equipment that is uncraftable or hasn't been found yet and no other mob has dropped any equipement in the current fight, one such worn equipment is dropped instead
*[[Raw Meat]] (40%)
*[[Bone]] (40%)
*[[Quiver]] (20%, only if no other equipment has been dropped yet in the current fight)
