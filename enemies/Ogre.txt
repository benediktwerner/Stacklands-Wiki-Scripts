'''Ogre''' is a [[:Category:Hostile Mobs|red]] [[Cards|Card]] in [[Stacklands]]. In the [[Cardopedia]], it is categorized as a [[:Category:Mobs|Mob]].

==How to Spawn==
*By visiting the [[Dark Forest]] (wave 4 or above)

==Possible Equipment==
*'''Hand''': [[Boomerang]], [[Morning Star]], [[Hammer]], [[Throwing Stars]], or [[Axe]]
*'''Body''': [[Leather Tunic]]

==Drops==
*If wearing equipment that is uncraftable or hasn't been found yet and no other mob has dropped any equipement in the current fight, one such worn equipment is dropped instead
*[[Bone]] (50%)
*[[Club]] (50%, only if no other equipment has been dropped yet in the current fight)
