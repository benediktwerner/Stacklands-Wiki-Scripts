'''Goblin''' is a [[:Category:Hostile Mobs|red]] [[Cards|Card]] in [[Stacklands]]. In the [[Cardopedia]], it is categorized as a [[:Category:Mobs|Mob]].

==How to Spawn==
*From [[Strange Portal]] or [[Rare Portal]] starting [[Moon]] 12

==Possible Equipment==
*'''Head''': [[Goblin Hat]]
*'''Hand''': [[Bone Spear]], [[Club]], [[Spiked Plank]], or [[Pickaxe]]
*'''Body''': [[Wooden Shield]]

==Drops==
*If wearing equipment that is uncraftable or hasn't been found yet and no other mob has dropped any equipement in the current fight, one such worn equipment is dropped instead
*[[Magic Dust]] (33%)
*[[Map]] (33%, only if no other equipment has been dropped yet in the current fight)
*[[Goblin Hat]] (33%, only if no other equipment has been dropped yet in the current fight)
