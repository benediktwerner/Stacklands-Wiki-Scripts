'''Orc Wizard''' is a [[:Category:Hostile Mobs|red]] [[Cards|Card]] in [[Stacklands]]. In the [[Cardopedia]], it is categorized as a [[:Category:Mobs|Mob]].

==How to Spawn==
*From ''Advanced Archipelago'' (20%) [[Card Packs]]
*By visiting the [[Dark Forest]] (wave 4 or above)

==Possible Equipment==
*'''Hand''': [[Staff of Fear]]
*'''Body''': [[Wizard Robe]]

==Drops==
*If wearing equipment that is uncraftable or hasn't been found yet and no other mob has dropped any equipement in the current fight, one such worn equipment is dropped instead
*[[Magic Dust]] (50%)
*[[Bone]] (50%)
