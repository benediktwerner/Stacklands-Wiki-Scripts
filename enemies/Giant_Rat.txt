'''Giant Rat''' is a [[:Category:Hostile Mobs|red]] [[Cards|Card]] in [[Stacklands]]. In the [[Cardopedia]], it is categorized as a [[:Category:Mobs|Mob]].

==How to Spawn==
*By exploring [[Catacombs]] (5%)
*From ''The Armory'' (21%) or ''Explorers'' (21%) [[Card Packs]]
*From [[Strange Portal]] or [[Rare Portal]] starting [[Moon]] 16

==Possible Equipment==
*'''Hand''': [[Bone Spear]], [[Spiked Plank]], [[Slingshot]], or [[Bow]]
*'''Body''': [[Wooden Shield]]

==Drops==
*If wearing equipment that is uncraftable or hasn't been found yet and no other mob has dropped any equipement in the current fight, one such worn equipment is dropped instead
*[[Raw Meat]] (33%)
*[[Bone]] (33%)
*[[Rat Crown]] (33%, only if no other equipment has been dropped yet in the current fight)
