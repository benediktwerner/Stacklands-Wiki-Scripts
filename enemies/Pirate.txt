'''Pirate''' is a [[:Category:Hostile Mobs|red]] [[Cards|Card]] in [[Stacklands]]. In the [[Cardopedia]], it is categorized as a [[:Category:Mobs|Mob]].

==How to Spawn==
*From ''Advanced Archipelago'' (18%) [[Card Packs]]
*By visiting the [[Dark Forest]] (wave 4 or above)

==Possible Equipment==
*'''Hand''': [[Blunderbuss]], [[Pirate Sabre]], or [[Slingshot]]
*'''Body''': [[Tiger Fur Coat]]

==Drops==
*If wearing equipment that is uncraftable or hasn't been found yet and no other mob has dropped any equipement in the current fight, one such worn equipment is dropped instead
*[[Coin]] (60%)
*[[Pirate Sabre]] (20%, only if no other equipment has been dropped yet in the current fight)
*[[Pirate Hat]] (20%, only if no other equipment has been dropped yet in the current fight)
